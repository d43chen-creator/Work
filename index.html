01 project

I redesigned the branding for Amsterdam Brewery, a Canadian beer company that needed a fresh touch. For the logo, I incorporated the CN Tower to represent the company’s Toronto roots and I added a star on top to pay tribute to the original logo’s design.

For the packaging, I retained the existing color scheme to maintain brand recognition but I also introduced subtle background elements, such as wave patterns, to represent the sense of being on a watercraft, aligning with the brand’s nautical theme.

02 project

Replica.ai is a financial app designed by my group during our design jam. The app is designed to help manage younger individuals finances. We decided to design our app in Figma. For my contribution, I helped design the layout and the pie charts. In the layout, I spaced out the content so that the elements can be individually visible and organized. For the pie charts, I selected different shades of greens to match the logo and background. The variety of shades help enhance the brands identity, bringing out the easiness and reliable nature.
